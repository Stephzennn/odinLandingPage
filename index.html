Wood types used in our furniture
We use Mahogany wood in our furniture when we need to emphasize a minimalist design.
Walnut is a hardwood, it can withstand daily wear and tear, making it suitable for furniture that needs to last.
MDF provides a cost-effective, smooth, and customizable solution for various woodworking projects.
Pine wood is a versatile choice for various applications, from furniture to flooring. Its a popular option for home projects.
Oak wood, whether red or white, offers a blend of strength, distinctive grain, and resistance to warping. It is also moisture-resistant and better suited for outdoor furniture.
Maple has a pale color with an even, tight wood grain. Its sapwood ranges from white to cream with red and brown hues. This wood type is perfect for indoor furniture.
